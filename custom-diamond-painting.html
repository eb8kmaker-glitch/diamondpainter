What It Is

What is custom diamond painting?

Custom diamond painting (커스텀 보석십자수) is a craft where you create a sparkling mosaic artwork from a photo of your choice. Small resin diamonds — called drills — are placed onto an adhesive canvas, position by position, following a color-coded pattern chart.

Unlike pre-made kits, a custom diamond painting uses your own personal photo — a beloved pet, a family portrait, a memorable landscape, or any image that matters to you. The result is a unique, handcrafted piece of art.

Diamond Painter converts your photo into a ready-to-use DMC color-coded pattern, so you know exactly which drill goes where — with bead counts per color for easy ordering.

Ideas

What can you create with custom diamond painting?

Any photo can become a stunning diamond art piece. Here are some popular subjects.

🐾

Pet Portraits

Turn your cat, dog, or any beloved pet into a sparkling diamond art portrait. High-contrast fur textures and expressive eyes convert beautifully.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family Photos

Create a meaningful keepsake from a treasured family photograph. A 40×50cm canvas works well for group portraits with 30–40 colors.

🌄

Landscapes

Dramatic sunsets, mountain vistas, and seascapes produce striking results. The natural color gradients map well to DMC drill palettes.

🎨

Artwork & Illustrations

Convert digital art, paintings, or illustrations into diamond painting patterns. Bold, graphic images with clear color areas work especially well.

🏠

Personalized Gifts

A custom diamond painting kit (pattern + ordered drills) makes a unique and heartfelt gift for birthdays, anniversaries, and holidays.

🌸

Flowers & Nature

Individual flowers, botanical subjects, and macro photography convert into vibrant, colorful patterns — perfect for smaller canvases.
