The Technology

How our generator produces accurate diamond art

The same color science used by professional diamond painting suppliers — available free in your browser.

Lab Color Space Analysis

Instead of working in RGB, the generator converts your image to CIE Lab color space — which models human color perception. This means color differences are calculated the way your eyes actually see them, not just mathematically.

K-Means Clustering

The generator uses k-means clustering to group similar colors in your image into the exact number of DMC colors you specify. The algorithm iterates until it finds the optimal set of colors that best represents your photo.

Perceptual DMC Matching

Each cluster center is matched to its closest DMC drill color using Delta-E distance in LCH color space. This ensures every color in your generated pattern has a real, purchasable DMC drill equivalent.

Floyd-Steinberg Dithering

Optional dithering distributes color quantization error across neighboring pixels, producing smoother gradients and more detail in the generated pattern — particularly noticeable in portraits and landscapes.

A diamond art generator is an online tool that takes any photo and automatically creates a diamond painting pattern from it.
