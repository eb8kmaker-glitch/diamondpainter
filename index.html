DMC (Dollfus-Mieg et Compagnie) is the world's most widely used thread and drill numbering system. Diamond painting drills are sold using the same codes, so DMC 321 always refers to the same shade of red across all major suppliers. This makes ordering the exact right colors straightforward.
Custom diamond painting is a craft where you recreate a personal photo as a mosaic artwork by placing small resin diamonds (drills) onto an adhesive canvas. A diamond painting pattern maker converts your photo into a DMC color-coded chart — every position on the canvas maps to a specific drill color, so you know exactly what to place where.
