Features

Everything you need to create a perfect pattern

Diamond Painter converts your image using professional-grade color quantization algorithms, the same technology used by commercial diamond painting suppliers.

🎨

AI Color Quantization

Our intelligent algorithm reduces your image to 5–80 perfectly matched DMC colors, preserving the essence of your photo while keeping the bead count manageable.

📐

Custom Canvas Sizes

Choose from standard sizes like A4, A3, 30×40cm, 40×50cm, or set your own custom dimensions. See bead counts and actual output size in real time.

🔢

DMC Color Codes

Every color is matched to its official DMC thread and drill code, so you know exactly which beads to order. Includes color names, hex values, and drill counts.

✏️

Manual Editing Tools

Refine your pattern with paint, erase, fill, and eyedropper tools. Undo and redo as many times as you need until the pattern looks exactly right.

◉

Round & Square Drills

Switch between round (2.8mm) and square (2.5mm) drills. Each type has different visual characteristics and the tool calculates spacing accordingly.

📤

Export & Save

Download your finished pattern as a high-resolution image, or save your work as a project file to continue editing later. Print-ready output included.
