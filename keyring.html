How to make a diamond painting keyring

  1. Prepare your image. Simple, bold images (characters, logos, illustrations) read best at keyring size.
  2. Choose your size, bead type, and number of colors.
  3. Compare the auto-generated candidates and pick the one you like.
  4. Fine-tune in the editor — repaint cells or swap colors (optional).
  5. Export a PDF or PNG, then assemble it with your physical materials.

What you'll need

The pattern is free to create and print on Diamond Painter. The physical materials are purchased separately:

  • Adhesive canvas blank (acrylic keyring blank)
  • DMC resin drills (buy the color numbers shown in your pattern)
  • Drill pen & tray
  • Keyring hardware (rings, chain)

We don't sell materials — Diamond Painter only makes the pattern. Any diamond painting drills and blanks will work.
