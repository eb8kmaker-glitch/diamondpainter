What Is Custom Diamond Art?

Custom diamond art is a diamond painting created from your own photo — not a pre-designed pattern, but your personal image transformed into a sparkling mosaic. You choose the photo, the canvas size, and the number of colors. The tool generates a precise DMC color chart showing exactly which resin diamonds to place at each position.

The result is a handcrafted piece of art that is uniquely yours — no one else in the world has the same diamond painting. That's what makes custom diamond art so popular as gifts, home décor, and personal creative projects.

How to Create Custom Diamond Art from a Photo

  • Step 1: Open Diamond Painter — no account or download needed
  • Step 2: Upload your chosen photo (JPG, PNG, or WebP)
  • Step 3: Pick your canvas size — A4, A3, 30×40cm, 40×50cm, or set custom dimensions
  • Step 4: Set the color count (25–40 is a good range for most photos)
  • Step 5: Click Generate — your DMC color-coded diamond art pattern is ready instantly
  • Step 6: Download the pattern and either order a pre-printed canvas or use it with loose drills

All processing happens in your browser. Your photo never leaves your device, so there are no privacy concerns.

Tips for the Best Custom Diamond Art Results

  • Choose photos with good contrast — light subjects against dark backgrounds (or vice versa) produce the clearest patterns
  • Avoid very busy or complex images — simple compositions with a clear focal point translate better to diamond art
  • For portraits, use 30–40 colors — this captures enough skin tone variation to make faces recognizable
  • For landscapes, try 20–30 colors — fewer colors create a more painterly, artistic effect
  • Larger canvases = more detail — a 40×50cm canvas has significantly more beads and more resolution than a 20×30cm
  • Use the editing tools — Diamond Painter's paint and fill tools let you refine the pattern after generation
