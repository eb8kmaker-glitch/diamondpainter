Tips

Get the best results from your photo

Follow these guidelines for a diamond painting pattern that truly captures your photo.

Use High-Contrast Photos

Images with a clear subject against a distinct background convert best. Pet portraits, single-person shots, and landscapes with good lighting work particularly well.

Minimum 800×800 Pixels

Higher resolution photos give the algorithm more detail to work with. Most smartphone photos are more than sufficient. Avoid heavily compressed or blurry images.

Start with 25–40 Colors

This range produces recognizable patterns for most photos. Use fewer colors for simple images, more for complex portraits. Going above 50 colors rarely improves results.

Larger Canvas = More Detail

A 40×50cm canvas gives you more grid squares, so finer details are preserved. A4 canvases work well for simple images but may blur complex patterns.
